# Smoke Effects Folder

Add your custom smoke GIF here!

## Recommended File:
- **Name:** `smoke.gif`
- **Size:** 256x256px or 512x512px
- **Duration:** 0.5-1.5 seconds
- **Background:** Transparent
- **Color:** White or light gray

## Where to Find Smoke GIFs:
- **Giphy** - Search "smoke effect transparent"
- **Tenor** - Search "realistic smoke"
- **Custom creation** - Use After Effects or Blender

## How to Use:
1. Place your smoke.gif in this folder
2. Update `src/components/UI/SmokeEffect.js` to use GIF mode
3. Follow instructions in `EFFECTS_GUIDE.md`

Your smoke effect will trigger on every button click!
