=====================================================
EFFECTS FOLDER - Your custom sound/video files
=====================================================

REQUIRED FILES:
---------------
1. gunshot.mp3
   - Short revolver gunshot sound
   - Plays on every click + loader bullets

2. reload.mp3
   - Revolver cylinder reload/spin sound
   - Plays while logo spins (2.5 seconds)

3. Bullet-Hole-Glass.mov
   - Bullet hole video effect
   - Shows at every click position
   - Stays visible for 5 seconds then fades

4. gunsmoke.mov
   - Smoke effect video
   - Plays once at click position


HOW IT WORKS:
-------------
1. User clicks "New Start" button
2. Logo spins with reload.mp3 playing
3. 6 bullets fire with gunshot.mp3 each
4. Site loads - every click creates:
   - Bullet-Hole-Glass.mov (stays 5 seconds)
   - gunsmoke.mov (plays once)
   - gunshot.mp3 sound
